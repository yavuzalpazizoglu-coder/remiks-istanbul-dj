RemiksBox — Privacy notice

This page describes how RemiksBox (DJ request & voting) processes data. Event organisers remain responsible for their own privacy notices toward attendees.

Controller

Contact: info@remiksistanbul.com

Data

  • Event operation: event name/status, song requests (title, artist, optional artwork URL), vote counts, a pseudonymous device identifier for per-device limits (stored in the browser).
  • DJ accounts: email-based user records where configured.
  • Optional: organiser logo file if uploaded.

Purpose

Providing the service during live events; legal bases include contract/legitimate interests and participation via QR.

Rights (GDPR / KVKK)

Contact the email above for access, rectification, erasure requests or objections.

General information only — have counsel review for production use.
